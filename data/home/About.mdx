Zifan Wang, a PhD student at the Department of Accounting, Business School, National University of Singapore, holds a Master's in Accountancy from Nanyang Technological University and Bachelor's degrees in Management and Economics from the Central University of Economics and Finance.

Prior to his doctoral studies, Zifan worked as a senior associate on the Global Investment Banking Management Committee at CITIC Securities. He is a Certified Internal Auditor, a Certified Information Systems Auditor, holds the Fundamentals of Sustainability Accounting Credential, is an Affiliate of the Association of Chartered Certified Accountants, and a member of the China Institution of Internal Audit.
