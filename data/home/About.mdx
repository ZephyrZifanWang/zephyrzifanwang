Zifan Wang is a PhD student at the Department of Accounting, part of the esteemed Business School at the National University of Singapore. He holds a Master's degree in Accountancy from Nanyang Technological University, and Bachelor's degrees in Management and Economics from the Central University of Economics and Finance.

Before pursuing his doctoral studies, Zifan worked as a senior associate on the Global Investment Banking Management Committee at CITIC Securities. He is a Certified Internal Auditor, a Certified Information Systems Auditor, holds the Fundamentals of Sustainability Accounting Credential, and is an Affiliate of the Association of Chartered Certified Accountants. Additionally, he is a member of the China Institution of Internal Audit.
